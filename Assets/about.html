About Us

We aim to become the central information hub for funding opportunities, scholarship application assistance, networking and support for African students, early career researchers and young scholars who want to further their education. Our mission

This is a free online platform designed to connect students like you with scholarship opportunities that fit your goals and values, with the ultimate goal of providing you with the resources to fund your higher education.

Our team here is committed to not only helping provide students with the opportunity to earn money to help them pay for school, but also to be a go-to resource for students to utilize for all things education-related (think, picking classes, navigating college life, and more!).
