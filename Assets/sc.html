Moringa Access Program (6 months full-time

The Access Program aims to bring Moringa’s
world-class education to ambitious youth
who would otherwise be excluded
from the program due to their financial circumstances.
We partner with organizations and
other well-wishers to provide full scholarships
and additional psychosocial support to applicants
who qualify to enter into Moringa but come
from low-income backgrounds.

Qualifications

Able to commit to the program full time for 6 months.
Students will have to attend class from
Monday to Friday 8:30am-6:00pm.

Type of Scholarship

This a fully paid scholarship
for a software development certificate

How To Apply

Qualified Students can apply through the school webiste
