Moringa Access Program (6 months full-time)

The Access Program aims to bring Moringa’s
world-class education to ambitious youth
who would otherwise be excluded
from the program due to their financial circumstances.
We partner with organizations and
other well-wishers to provide full scholarships
and additional psychosocial support to applicants
who qualify to enter into Moringa but come
from low-income backgrounds..

Qualifications

  • Able to differentiate between
    software and hardware
    and what each does
  • Understands how Microsoft office works
  • passionate about technology
  • Able to navigate through an internet browser
  • Able to commit to the program full time for 6 months.

Type of Scholarship

This a fully paid scholarship
for a software development certificate

How To Apply

Qualified Students can apply through the school webiste

The Aga Khan Academy Mombasa

The Aga Khan Academy Mombasa is launching
a unique scholarship programme to attract Kenya’s
brightest young minds to join the Academy.
Six merit-based scholarships of up to
50% off fees for outstanding students who can
demonstrate superior academic ability.

Qualifications

  • To apply, students must be residents in Kenya.
  • B average for international curricula
  • B+ for KCSE students
  • 390+ for KCPE
  • The scholarships are open to students who are able to
    demonstrate exceptional academic ability and the potential to
    benefit from a unique educational programme based on the
    International Baccalaureate and the Aga Khan Strands.

Type of Scholarship

Up to 50% of school fees, including residential fees.

How To Apply

Students should make an application through the
normal admissions process (an application fee is payable)
and indicate their interest in applying
for the Aga Khan Academy Scholarship Programme.
